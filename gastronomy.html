Gastronomy Account Management

This service was provided to a gastronomy dedicated account (@degustasc) which aim is to promote local cafes and restaurants from Santa Cruz de Tenerife, Spain. I took pictures during the International Potato Forum, organised by Santa Cruz’s public institutions, including our client, and post them on Instagram Stories. Also, I covered the whole two-day event through their Twitter profile.

For the editing of the pictures I used mobile editing apps, such as Unfold Stories Editor and Hyperlapse. Using the first one, I framed pictures and created collages to mix two images in one story, so the content would be creative and dynamic. For the videos, I filmed them in time-lapse, so they would not last more than the 15 seconds established by the format of Instagram Stories. As there can be seen in this portfolio, I combined sped-up videos of food prepping with a picture of the final result.

The feedback received from the client was really positive and charming, since they really enjoyed the way I covered the event, with special attention to visual details.

All videos and pictures were taken from an iPhone 8 Plus by Yanira González.
