Trend Alert: Winter Edition!

My Blog


December 13, 2019
Hi everyone! Since winter season has arrived, I thought it would be nice to share some of my favourite pieces of what is trending for this upcoming cold and snowy months. I’ve always loved rainy and chilly weather - even though I’m from an island where the sun is almost out everyday. But specially I’ve loved dressing up with cozy and cute clothes. Some of my all-time favourite pieces are neutral tones than match each other and can be worn in every season regardless of the trends.

Layers over layers
Undoubtly, coats are a must this winter, specially with low temperatures and rain. If I’m honest, this is my favourite piece of clothing. I own coats from every colour and size, but my go-to one is a large camel coat. For this season, plaid and black are essentials in your wardrobe. Also, you will never go wrong with a brown or faux fur one!

My favourites are brown shearling and plaid masculine coats.

Green everywhere

I have to say I was not a huge fan of this colour until I started seeing it in every piece of clothes I found cute and flattering. From an iPhone case to a baggy jumper, you will see pistaccio green everywhere you look at when going shopping. So, during this winter you will bright with pastel and colorful green tones.

Some of my favourite green pieces are turtleneck jumpers and slouchy jeans.

Snake skin boots

For this season, animal print is all over again on shoes. This time was the snake’s turn to become one of the most popular trends of the winter. Honestly, I prefer crocodile print on shoes and bags, but snake skin has caught my attention too!

My favourites are natural snake skin ones.

Head-to-toe camel

I have to admit that I am a huge fan of this trend where you can dress from your jumper to your boots in camel tones and still look like you spent a quite time working on your outfit. Usually I wear neutral tones during this cozy season because is more comfortable and practical to me, but since it is becoming a thing I’d have to practice it more often.

Some of my favourite pieces that can make a look all together are a fluffy camel jumper with brown slouchy jeans and a large trench coat paired with some tan animal printed boots.

Back in time

Though it was not so long ago, bucket hats and bermuda shorts are back on track. The most worn ones are made from leather but you can find them from other materials too. This combination made popular in the 90s can make your look more casual and comfy while trendy and stylish. I have to say that I have not followed this trend yet, but some bucket hats have stolen my heart.

Some of my favourites shorts are the black faux leather ones and for bucket hats I'm loving the brown tones.

Chunky boots

Yes, luckily they are trending this season too. For those who want to be dressed up while walking comfortable, this is your pair of boots. I remember I got one pair of burgundy military boots about six years ago and then, last year, got in my hands some black ones that have really saved my life when coming with an outfit. I have worn them during summer in my home island and during winter in London, and they go along both with warm and cool weather.

Some of my favourites boots from this season are the black shiny Dr. Martens and high chunky ones.

I hope you enjoyed my personal choices! Stay tuned for more articles. Thank you!

Love, Yanira
