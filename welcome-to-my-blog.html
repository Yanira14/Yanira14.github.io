My Blog


October 26, 2019


Hi everyone! This is Yanira González and I´m excited to welcome you to my personal blog. Through this platform I will share my opinions, thoughts and interests about the fashion industry for you to get to know me better.

I came up with this idea some time ago but I was not sure if blogging would be the perfect choice so it took me time to create a website that shows how I am (in a design way) and finally it has seen the light.

Just a little background

I was born in the Canary Islands and most of my life I have felt like fashion was the best way to show my personality to the world. In high school, I would always wear my dad’s vintage clothes and buy in thrift shops for every vintage piece I would like. Growing up, I found my passion in writing about the things I enjoyed the most, as fashion, films and books, so I decided to become a journalist.

One of the main reasons I wanted to go to the University was to study abroad through the Erasmus+ programme. Even though I grew up being obsessed with the United Kingdom and the british culture, I studied in Poland and it was the best decision I’ve ever made! I got to travel to almost all of European countries and meet people from all around the world.

Now, I recently graduated and I am not quite sure about what I will become in the next five years but I have one thing clear: I want to focus in whatever makes me happy. I know for sure that I will enjoy working for the fashion industry (maybe even working in my own brand someday!) but I still have to try in which departments I will feel more comfortable in.

So, I hope you stay tuned for what it’s coming to the blog and enjoy the content I have prepared for this platform. Thank you so much for reading this and see you in my next post!

Love, Yanira
